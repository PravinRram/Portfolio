2023

CCA Fair is an event where every CCA can showcase their content to Sec 1 students to encourage them to join a CCA. I helped by making materials and manning the booths to promote Infocomm Club.

Attended the IMDA Leadership in Action Workshop on Tech for Good and Emerging Technologies 2023

The opportunity was given to all Infocomm members to participate in this workshop. I opted to join the workshop so that I could learn about leadership qualities and how I can use my skills in IT for good. I was inspired from the activities during the workshop to make a change in my CCA.

Participated in Drone Odyssey Challenge 2023 by Science Centre Singapore

This competition tests on participants' abilities to code their drone on the spot based off the given obstacle course and successfully complete as many obstacles as possible. I participated with my team in the B2 Category and obtained a Certificate of Participation.

Elected as Infocomm Club President / Chairperson 2023 - 2024

Learnt Game Development through block coding on the Microsoft Makecode Platform

Learning game development was a welcomed shift in what members of the CCA had been learning since they joined Infocomm. This was achieved as I and the EXCO had encouraged our Teachers-In-Charge to find vendors teaching more relevant and current IT skills. I had learnt that game development can be relatively simple and that it requires many factors to make a good game.

Attended Learning Journey by IMDA to ARTC@A*STAR in 2023

This learning journey taught me about fields in IT (AR/VR) that I was not very familiar with. I learnt how AR/VR is used in work environments. I saw how robots and automation systems were used to perform tasks in manufacturing with better control, efficiency and quality.I also saw the kinds of advanced manufacturing and remanufacturing technologies that are being developed in the institute.

Organised an inter-CCA programme called 'Bonding Day' with Sec 2s and 3s to teach Dunman Secondary Admin Staff about simple video editing

Bonding Day was an event that aimed to bring the Sec 2s and 3s closer together as a connected CCA. This activity was one of the activities that we had planned for the day. We wanted to give back to our school so we used our IT skills for the benefit of those in our community. We chose to do video editing as it is simple enough for not tech-savvy people to pick up. The sec 2s and 3s helped the admin staff well while the lesson was ongoing to ensure that they can learn to the best of their abilties. We had pciked up invaluable communication and time management skills from this activity. I am the one who is taking the picture shown here.

Organised with the EXCO an inter-CCA coding competition during Bonding Day

After teaching the Admin staff about video editing, we moved on to the next activity, which was a coding competition. We chose to test the CCA members about their knowledge with the EV3 Lego Mindstorms, as it was something that everyone across the levels had a chance to learn. We encouraged members to mix and group up with others they are unfamiliar with during the competition. We had a exciting race to see who could finish the course the EXCO made the fastest, and members worked well with each other to do their best.

Organise a programme with sec 2s, sec 3s, Teachers-In-Charge, and External Organisations like VIVITA to teach primary school students about robot coding and simple game development as part of 'Tech Saturday' programme by VIVITA at Heartbeat@Bedok

When I went to the leadership workshop by IMDA earlier in the year, I was inspired and taught that we could arrange with the organisations that promoted their services at the workshop to hold a VIA project for all Infocomm members. We decided to do the Tech Saturday programme with VIVITA. Using the skills we learnt from previous CCA sessions, we chose to teach the participants 2 IT skills as there was ample time. Half of the CCA worked on the game development, and the other on robot coding. I was leading the robot coding segment. I worked with my group to come up with a lesson plan that can teach the children about the relevant skills needed to use the EV3 Lego Mindstorms robots. We held a small challenge at the end to test their newfound skills. We had to work very closely with multiple stakeholders to ensure this event was a success.

I played a part of ensuring that our team was following and documenting the process of data analysis (data cleaning, data profiling, data visualisation, etc.)
