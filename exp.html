Infocomm Club (2021 - 2024)

2021

Learnt EV3 Lego Mindstorms

We learnt block coding by using the Lego Mindstorms Education EV3 app to code the robots and built specialised robots from Lego.

2022

Learnt Mobile App Development in SwiftUI

We used the Playgrounds app our PLDs (iPads) to learn about Mobile App Development. These lessons were not taught to all Sec 2s at that time as they were meant for the Sec 1s. Moreover, the Sec 2s were going to continue learning Lego Mindstorms. Thus, I opted to join them to get a taste of text-based coding and a new field of IT.

2023

Participated in CCA Fair 2023

CCA Fair is an event where every CCA can showcase their content to Sec 1 students to encourage them to join a CCA. I helped by making materials and manning the booths to promote Infocomm Club.

Learnt Drone Flying and Drone Coding

We learnt how to fly the DJI Ryze Tech Tello Edu Drone Manually, then proceeded to learn about how to fly the drone through block coding on the DJI Ryze Tech Tello Edu App on our PLDs. This was in preparation for our upcoming competition.

Attended the IMDA Leadership in Action Workshop on Tech for Good and Emerging Technologies 2023

The opportunity was given to all Infocomm members to participate in this workshop. I opted to join the workshop so that I could learn about leadership qualities and how I can use my skills in IT for good. I was inspired from the activities during the workshop to make a change in my CCA.

Participated in Drone Odyssey Challenge 2023 by Science Centre Singapore

This competition tests on participants' abilities to code their drone on the spot based off the given obstacle course and successfully complete as many obstacles as possible. I participated with my team in the B2 Category and obtained a Certificate of Participation.

Elected as Infocomm Club President / Chairperson 2023 - 2024

All members of the Infocomm Club had a chance in choosing who they wanted as the new EXCO members and including the opinions of the Teachers-In-Charge, I was elected as President, along with the other members like Vice-President and Level-Heads as part of the EXCO for 2023 to 2024./p>

Attened Learning Journey by IMDA to ARTC@A*STAR in 2023

This learning journey taught me about fields in IT (AR/VR) that I was not very familiar with. I learnt how AR/VR is used in work environments. I saw how robots and automation systems were used to perform tasks in manufacturing with better control, efficiency and quality.I also saw the kinds of advanced manufacturing and remanufacturing technologies that are being developed in the institute.
